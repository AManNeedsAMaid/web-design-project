Recommended Materials

Here you can find our list of materials, curated and tested by our expert staff.

We curate suggestions for:
  1. Paper
  2. Scissors
  3. Origami Tools
  4. Paper Trimmers
  5. Rulers
  6. Paper Cases
  • Paper:

    TANT Thick Origami Paper

    Thicker paper than most, but comes in vibrant colors and makes delicate designs stand stronger.


  • Scissors:

    Kai 5165-WC 6 1/2 Inch Scissors

    Kai scissors are the brand to go for, and these offer a good balance of size and price for origami. Feel free to go with a bigger or smaller model depending on your preference.


  • Origami Tools

    Vencink Origami Paper Creaser

    A simple tool to help you fold origami paper. Especially useful for people with accessibility concerns.


  • Paper Trimmer

    Cricut Portable Paper Trimmer

    Precision paper trimmer, and portable enough to travel with!


  • Ruler

    Westcott 10415 Stainless Metal Ruler

    If this classic ruler can withstand decades of abuse in American public schools, it can withstand your origami needs!


  • Paper Case

    LuPro Origami Folding Paper Case Box

    A plastic container which holds origami paper. Especially useful to both travel with and keep your paper safe.
